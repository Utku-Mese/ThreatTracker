About Us

About Us

As the Thread Tracker team, we set out to make a difference in the world of cybersecurity. The increasing global cyberattacks and the threats they pose are increasingly endangering the security of individuals and organizations alike. Therefore, we have created an innovative platform that monitors and analyzes cyberattacks occurring worldwide.

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about cybersecurity and provide our users with better protection against cyber threats. With our cyberattack monitoring and analysis tools, we help users take proactive measures by visualizing where cyberattacks are coming from and where they are targeting in real-time.

Our Vision

Our vision is to spread cybersecurity awareness worldwide and become a leading platform in creating a safer digital environment. In a world where technology is rapidly evolving, we aim to strengthen our users' security strategies by constantly innovating and providing the most up-to-date data.

Our Values

Innovation: We continuously develop our cybersecurity solutions by using the latest technologies and methods. Reliability: We aim to be a reliable source of information by providing our users with accurate and trustworthy data. Transparency: By sharing transparent information about cyber threats and attacks, we enable our users to make informed decisions. Education: We offer educational materials and resources to increase awareness about cybersecurity. Collaboration: By promoting global cybersecurity collaborations, we aim to combat cybercrime more effectively.

Our Services

Monitoring and Analyzing Cyberattacks: We examine and analyze cyberattacks occurring worldwide. Interactive Visualization: We visualize the source and target points of attacks on a world map using dynamic lines. By interacting with these lines, users can obtain more detailed information about the attack. Regional Attack Analysis: We provide graphics detailing large-scale attacks targeting specific regions. Education and Information: We provide users with information and educational materials on cybersecurity.

At Thread Tracker, we are here to ensure security in the digital world and provide effective solutions against cyber threats. Aligned with our mission and vision, we stand by our users and always prioritize their security.
